Crebel Homes - Site Plan

Site Purpose

The purpose of Crebel Homes is to provide a simple and effective platform for users to find, view and inquire about properties sale or rent. The site will offer a detailed property listings, high-quality images, location maps and agent contact information. It will also include search and filter tools to help users find homes that match their needs.

Scenarios

As a first-time homebuyer, how easily can you search for availabe properties within your budget and preferred location on the Crebel Homes site?

As a real estate agent, how effectively can you list new properties and coonect with potential buyers or renters through the site's conatct features?
